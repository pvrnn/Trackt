06 · Texture recipe
AURA LAYER
Fixed full-viewport layer of 3–4 large radial-gradients (violet top-left, pink right, gold bottom), each fading to transparent by 65–70%. Opacity 0.25–0.35 on app pages; up to 0.55 on marketing/login panels.
GRAIN LAYER
noise.svg (fractal turbulence, desaturated) tiled at 220px, opacity 0.5, mix-blend-mode overlay, on top of the aura. Never on top of text containers — it sits between background and content.
07 · Voice
DO
Short, declarative, second person. "Your data, forever." "Checked in." "186 episodes this year." Numbers do the bragging.
DON'T
No exclamation marks, no emoji, no "awesome!". Never guilt-trip about dropped shows — dropping is a first-class status.
